Kevin Harden
Owner · Tattoo Artist · Anchorage, Alaska · Since 1992

Growing up, Kevin was always drawing — took every art class in school. Raised on Saturday morning cartoons, Mad Magazine, comic books, Big Daddy Ed Roth model kits, Star Wars & cheesy low budget 80's movies.

Favorite Artists
Arthur Adams, Alphonse Mucha, Moebius, Frank Frazetta, Bill Sienkiewicz, William Stout, Skottie Young, Joe Mad, Dale Keown, Berni Wrightson, Giger, Johfra Bosschart

Favorite Tattoo Artists
Filip Leu, Tim Biedron, Aaron Cain, Guy Aitchison, Grime, Patrick Conlon, James Tex, Steve Moore, Kaija Heitland, Markus Lenhard

Kevin started his tattoo journey January 1992, apprenticing under Temple Drake @ Tattoo Technique in Bremerton, Washington. Over 34 years later, he's still at it — and still loves every minute.

Kevin specializes in Bio-Organica (his signature Organic Mech style), and surreal nature-based imagery. Biomechanical. Neo-Traditional. His technique is heavily influenced by Alphonse Mucha and the Art Nouveau movement.

AK Tattoo is not your average shop. Walk in and you'll find original paintings, sculptures, and one-of-a-kind custom tattoo designs covering the walls. This is an artist's studio — Kevin's clients have included Nikki Sixx of Mötley Crüe and Mike Herrera, lead singer of MxPx.

Frequently Asked Questions
How much does a tattoo cost? +
Pricing depends on size, placement, and complexity. Smaller pieces typically start around $75–$150. To get an honest estimate, come into the studio so we can go over all the details and get a full understanding of your needs and budget.
Do you accept walk-ins? +
Yes — walk-ins are welcome. Come by 409 W. Northern Lights Blvd, Suite 2, Anchorage, AK 99503. For larger custom pieces, booking an appointment is recommended. Fill out the Contact/Tattoo Info Form on the front page.
How should I prepare for my tattoo? +
Eat a full meal within 2 hours beforehand. Stay hydrated the day before and day of. Wear clothing that gives easy access to the area being tattooed. Skip alcohol for 24+ hours — it thins the blood. Get good sleep.
What is the aftercare process? +
Keep the wrap on for 4–12 hours. Wash gently with unscented soap and pat dry. Moisturize with Aquaphor or unscented lotion 2–3 times daily for 2 weeks. Stay out of pools and hot tubs for 2–3 weeks. Avoid direct sun while healing. Don't pick or scratch — let it flake naturally. Full healing takes about 4 weeks.
What styles does Kevin specialize in? +
Neo-Traditional: Bold linework, rich color, built to age well.

Bio-Organica: Kevin's organic collage style — bones, skulls, sea life, shells, wood, and natural textures that flow with the body.

Everything is drawn specifically for you — small pieces to full back projects. Kevin also has a wall full of designs and several books of his own original artwork, typically offered at a slight discount. Come hang out and look at the artwork.
How do I book an appointment? +
Call (907) 365-9410 or stop by 409 W. Northern Lights Blvd, Suite 2, Anchorage, AK 99503. Walk-ins are welcome. For custom or large-scale projects, fill out the Contact Info Form.
Does getting a tattoo hurt? +
Yes, to varying degrees — but very manageable. Pain depends on placement and personal tolerance. Areas over bone (ribs, spine) are more intense. Fleshy areas like the upper arm are more comfortable. Being well-rested, hydrated, and fed makes a big difference.
Is AK Tattoo clean and safe? +
Absolutely. AK Tattoo uses single-use sterile needles and follows industry-standard sanitation protocols. Clean, professional, relaxed — no chaos, no pressure.
